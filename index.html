COMP2013 project: PoliticsMapper

Team: Anuz, Reece & Esben

Our project is about analysing historical data from the parliament. We segment debates and meta data about these, MPs, and so on. The user is then able to find a segment and find semantically related segments in other parts of the dataset. The goal is to find out what kind of insights a end-user can get from this tool.
